Welcome to Re-Energise

Re-Energise is an energy management system that helps you to monitor and track your energy usage. The system is designed to help you identify & reduce your energy costs, minimise your carbon footprint, and improve your overall energy efficiency.

The Features

Usage Tracking

With Re-Energise, you can track your energy usage for specific appliances, allowing you to identify areas where you can reduce your consumption and save money on your energy bills.

Budget Limits

Re-Energise allows you to set budget costs that will provide you with a display on how far you have used up your budget. This feature helps you to stay within your energy budget and avoid unexpected costs.

Advice & Tips

Re-Energise not only helps you track your energy usage for specific appliances but also provides valuable advice and tips on reducing your energy consumption and saving money on your energy bills.

Community Forum

Re-Energise provides a community forum feature where you can discuss energy-saving strategies or anything with other users. This feature allows you to reply to posts and comments, exchange ideas, and collaborate with other users.
